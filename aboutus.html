Who are we??

This project is a brainchild of three young women We are in the penultimate year of our college. Having spent most of our college life at home in quarantine all of us at some point of time felt that we could have used some resource, app or a website which could help us brighten our mood instantly. An epty mind is a devil's workshop, with a lots of spare time on our hands and while being confined to our homes only going got really hard at times.When we, being completely healthy and privileged had such difficulties, it's really hard to imagine what those people with mental conditions or mental health issues must have gone through.

This project is an attempt from ourside to provide people with some resources that will ight help them feel better in the moment or atleast divert their minds for sometime
